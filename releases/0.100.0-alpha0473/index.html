Self-Healing Engine for Selenium

Self-healing can repair broken selectors at run-time. Greatly simplifying development and making your scripts more robust.

AutoWait

AutoWait will fix those flaky test scenarios. AutoWait will intelligently perform a series of retry operations before the Self-Healing Engine kicks in. AutoWait is a real time saver.

Selector Optimization Engine

The Selector Optimization Engine will analyze every Selector at run-time and provide optimization suggestions.

Self-Healing Insights Engine

Our Self-Healing Insights Engine, monitors the state of your Selenium Automation and brings this information together in one place to action. If you run large automation suites, this project integrates directly with your CI system.
